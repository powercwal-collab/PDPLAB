# PDP Page Structure Template

Use this file when planning or improving page structure.

## Recommended Sequence

| order | module | screen length | task |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | 封面故事/KV | 0.5-1屏 | 让用户停下，并在3秒内知道这是什么、强在哪里、属于谁 |
| 2 | 沉浸式购物/场景化 | N屏 | 让用户想象自己在真实场景中使用或穿着 |
| 3 | 产品卖点/功能证明 | 1-3屏 | 把参数翻译成利益点，并证明它有效 |
| 4 | 细节查阅 | 1-2屏 | 支持用户细看、比较、确认品质 |
| 5 | 基础信息/对比选购 | 0.5-1.5屏 | 降低理解成本，帮助用户判断哪款、哪码、哪色适合自己 |
| 6 | 使用说明/服务事项 | 1屏内 | 解决临门一脚的风险顾虑 |
| 7 | 关联推荐 | 1屏内 | 把单品购买扩展为搭配、系列和场景购买 |
| 8 | 品牌/产品背书 | 0.5-1屏 | 用可信证据支撑价值感和溢价 |

## Structure Judgment

High-scoring PDPs do not become strong by getting longer. They become strong when every module answers a purchase question.

Common structure problems:

- Hero is strong, but quick decision information is buried.
- Scene images are beautiful but not tied to benefits.
- Selling points are numerous but not ranked.
- Size and fit information appears late or lacks advice.
- Page ending lacks recommendation, comparison, or trust closure.

## Quick Redesign Sequence

For most product pages, recommend:

`封面KV -> 一屏看懂 -> 核心卖点 -> 功能证明 -> 场景穿着/使用 -> 细节查阅 -> 尺码/适配 -> 服务说明 -> 关联推荐 -> 品牌背书`

## Kids Performance Shoes Sequence

Use this sequence for children's basketball shoes, running shoes, training shoes, and toddler sport shoes when parents need fast reassurance and product choice guidance:

`主图视频/封面 -> 年龄段&核心卖点 -> 怎么挑/运动等级 -> 卖点拆解 -> 场景穿着 -> 上脚效果 -> 多色呈现 -> 产品信息/尺码 -> 测量方法 -> 系列推荐`

Recommended main-image carousel:

| frame | module | decision task |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | 主图视频/封面 | establish product identity, age stage, and strongest claim |
| 2 | 年龄段&核心卖点 | clarify who it is for and why parents should keep reading |
| 3 | 卖点拆解 | translate shoe-part features into safety, comfort, and performance benefits |
| 4 | 穿着场景 | show indoor/outdoor sport context and child movement |
| 5 | 穿着效果 | show on-foot and full-body outfit effect |
| 6 | 多色呈现 | connect color choice, nickname, and on-foot imagination |
| 7-8 | 细节卖点 | show outsole, tongue, upper, heel, toe, lining, or other risk-reducing details |

Structure judgment for kids performance shoes:

- Strong: parents can decide age stage, scenario, sport level, size direction, and main safety/comfort benefits within the first two screens or main-image sequence.
- Medium: the information exists, but parents must scroll several screens or infer the benefit from visual assets.
- Weak: the page is mostly product angles, color thumbnails, or slogans without parent-facing decision logic.
